Useful Resources

Why should I use SynergyRecycle.B?

+

Our platform rewards you for correct recycling with utility tokens (SRB), allows you to track your environmental impact, and support global green projects, making sustainability easy and rewarding.

How do I earn SRB tokens?

+

You earn SRB tokens primarily by recycling correctly through partner collection points and registering your actions via our app. Other opportunities might include participating in special initiatives or staking (locking) tokens.

How can I use SRB tokens?

+

SRB tokens can be used to:

  • Get discounts or services from affiliated partners.
  • Support specific environmental projects within the platform.
  • Trade them on decentralized (DEX) or centralized (CEX) exchanges once listed.
  • Access premium ecosystem features (in the future).

Where can I find partner collection points?

+

The map of partner collection points will be available within our mobile app (coming soon). We are working to expand our network.

What is SynergyRecycle.B?

SynergyRecycle.B is an innovative project combining blockchain and sustainability. Our goal is to incentivize recycling and environmental protection through a reward system based on utility tokens.

What security measures are adopted?

+

We adopt secure blockchain technologies (Polygon), smart contracts verified through external audits, and best practices for protecting user data and funds.

What is a blockchain?

+

A blockchain is a distributed, immutable, and transparent digital ledger that records transactions (or data) in cryptographically linked blocks. Each new block strengthens the security of the previous ones.

What does ICO (Initial Coin Offering) mean?

+

An ICO is a fundraising method in the crypto world, similar to an IPO in the stock market. A new project sells its native tokens (like SRB) to the public to fund development.

What is a cryptocurrency wallet?

+

A cryptocurrency wallet is a digital tool (software or hardware) that allows you to interact with the blockchain: send, receive, and store your cryptocurrencies securely by managing your private keys.

What are the risks of investing in cryptocurrencies?

+

Cryptocurrency investments are subject to significant risks, including high price volatility, regulatory uncertainty, technological risks (bugs, hacks), custody risks, and the potential for total capital loss. It is crucial to do thorough research (DYOR - Do Your Own Research) and invest only what you are willing to lose.

What is Polygon (MATIC)?

+

Polygon is a scaling platform for Ethereum aimed at improving transaction speed and reducing costs (gas fees), making decentralized applications more efficient. SRB operates on the Polygon network.

Utility Token

A type of cryptocurrency designed to provide access to a specific product or service within a blockchain ecosystem.

Staking

The process of locking (staking) a certain amount of cryptocurrency in a wallet to support the operations of a blockchain network (validation, security) in exchange for rewards.

DEX (Decentralized Exchange)

A cryptocurrency exchange platform based on smart contracts that operates without a central intermediary, allowing peer-to-peer trading.

CEX (Centralized Exchange)

A cryptocurrency exchange platform managed by a central company (e.g., Binance, Coinbase) that facilitates trades between users.

Circular Economy

A regenerative economic model where materials are kept in use for as long as possible, extracting maximum value from them, and then recovering and regenerating them at the end of their life cycle.

Smart Contract

Self-executing digital contracts stored on a blockchain, with the terms of the agreement written directly into the code. They automatically execute actions when predefined conditions are met.
